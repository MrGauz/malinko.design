Hi, I’m Denis.

Multitasking, clarity, engagement, and diversity are essential for me. Aesthetics are just the final touch in a long chain of decisions. Below are the key processes and competencies that form the foundation of thoughtful and effective design.

Multimedia Approach

Confident work across both digital and analog mediums. Flexibility in tool selection, from hand-drawn visuals to interactive digital solutions — tailored to each project’s needs.

UX/UI

Experience designing user interfaces for both public sector and private clients. Completed a professional internship at the German digital agency AOE. Skilled in the full UX/UI cycle: user research, journey mapping, prototyping, interface design. Focused on creating intuitive, user-centered, and business-aligned solutions.

Brand Identity

Development of cohesive visual identities for small and medium businesses, educational institutions, and cultural organizations. A comprehensive approach to brand strategy and visual language that ensures clarity and recognizability.

Packaging Design

Creation of packaging that enhances product value and stands out on the shelf. Attention to both visual impact and tactile experience.

Editorial Design

Layout design for print and digital publications. Emphasis on typography, composition, structure, and visual rhythm. Posters, books, booklets, business cards, magazines — everything is fun.

Design Research

User and contextual research to inform design decisions. Systematic approach to data gathering, insight generation, and integration into the creative process.

Motion Design

Creation of animated assets and motion graphics to enhance digital communication. Experience with kinetic typography, animated branding, and interface motion — adding clarity, emotion, and rhythm to visual storytelling of any tipe and purpose.

Leadership & Project Management

Experience leading creative teams and managing projects from concept to delivery. Strong organisational skills, clear communication, and deadline discipline.
