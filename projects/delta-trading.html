Delta Trading

Logo, identity and website design for import and distribution company

Logo design | Brand identity | UX/UI

Delta Trading, a B2B company specialising in wholesale trade and logistics, received a comprehensive brand solution including a logo, visual identity, and website—designed to reflect both strategic focus and national identity. The logo is built around three key symbols: a target representing precision and focus on the product and audience (“What?”); a perimeter indicating niche and market scope (“Where?”); and directional arrows symbolizing logistical flexibility and delivery dynamics (“Where to?”). The visual language is enriched with ornamental elements inspired by traditional Ukrainian embroidery, referencing the brand’s geographic roots and cultural context. The website design emphasizes clear structure, business trust, and seamless navigation for B2B partners—reinforcing the company’s reliability and adaptability within modern supply chains.

Website: https://delta-trading.com.ua/en/
