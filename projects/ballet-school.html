Ballet school "Star Ballet"

Logo and corporate identity redesign project
for the ballet school in Mainz.

Logo design | Brand identity

The objective was to refresh the ballet school’s brand identity, creating a more striking, memorable, and conceptually meaningful visual representation. While ballet is traditionally seen as graceful and refined, at its core lies rigorous discipline, strength, and relentless dedication. This contrast—between elegance and inner toughness—became the foundation of the logo concept.

  • Four pairs of legs in the first position symbolize the beginning of a journey. They are intentionally depicted incomplete, representing students who are just starting to “stand on their own feet” in the world of ballet.
  • The rhythmic, symmetrical arrangement of the legs emphasizes the collaborative nature of ballet. Precision, trust, and teamwork are essential—true results come only through collective effort.
  • The star shape formed by the legs is a metaphor for discipline and structure. It represents the inner focus and determination required to reach the heights of success.
  • The star itself alludes to the ultimate goal: to become exceptional, to shine on stage, and to stand out in the profession.
  • The silhouette of a curved foot, subtly integrated into the outer rectangular shapes, adds an element of movement and grace. It nods to ballet’s visual beauty while maintaining the logo’s clean, strong graphic language.
